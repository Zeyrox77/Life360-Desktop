How do I get my access token?
  1. Open life360.com/login in this browser and sign in normally (email + the code).
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools and open the Network tab.
  3. Reload the page (F5) and click the request named manage-membership.
  4. Under Headers → Request Headers, scroll to Cookie and find LIFE360_AUTH_TOKEN=.
  5. Copy everything after the = up to (but not including) the next ;, and paste it above.

Tip: you can also paste the whole LIFE360_AUTH_TOKEN=...; chunk — it gets cleaned up automatically.

Your token is used only to talk to Life360 and is stored locally in this browser (never sent to or kept on any server).
